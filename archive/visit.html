Plan Your Visit

Everything you need to know — how to book, where to find us, what to expect on the tour, and how your attendance supports local charities.

Getting here

The Hiscox Building
Peasholme Green
York YO1 7PR

A twenty-minute walk from York train station and ten minutes from York Minster — in the Hungate district of the medieval city, beside the city walls.

By train: York station is served by LNER, CrossCountry and TransPennine Express.

By car: Parking is available at Monk Bar car park (YO31 7JL, 5 min walk) and Foss Bank (YO31 9JS, 8 min walk). The building is in the city-centre controlled zone.

On the tour

Duration: Approximately 90 minutes, covering all four floors of the building. Please arrive on time.

Charity: Most tours are supported by the charity and their volunteers will talk about the amazing work they do.

Donation & charity

The tours operate on a pay-what-you-can basis, and tickets are sold through ticketsource.

In 2026, all proceeds go to Guide Dogs UK, who provide life-changing guide dogs and other services to people living with sight loss across the UK.

The Hiscox Foundation match-funds donations collected on each tour, doubling the impact of every pound raised. Since 2018, these tours have raised over £35,000 for York charities.

Access

The building has step-free access throughout, including a lift to all floors. Guide dogs and assistance animals are welcome. If you have specific access requirements, please get in touch in advance so we can make appropriate arrangements.

The tour involves standing and walking for approximately 90 minutes. If you have mobility concerns, please let us know and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Upcoming dates

Tour dates for 2026 are being announced through the year. New dates typically sell out within days of announcement.

The best way to hear about new dates is to follow the Hiscox York Fundraising Arts Tours page on Facebook, or bookmark the TicketSource page below.

Group bookings and enquiries about private tours should be directed to hiscoxyorkartstours@gmail.com.
